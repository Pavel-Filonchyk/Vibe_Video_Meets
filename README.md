PRIVACY POLICY

for the Vibe Video Meets (VVM) mobile app

1. General Provisions
1.1. This Privacy Policy (hereinafter — the "Policy") is an integral part of the User Agreement of the mobile application "Vibe Video Meets" (hereinafter — the "Application") and defines the procedure for processing and protecting Users' personal data, as well as other information received by the Application Administration.
1.2. This Policy has been developed in accordance with the Law of the Republic of Belarus No. 99-3 dated May 7, 2021 "On Personal Data Protection" (hereinafter — the Law on Personal Data), as well as other regulatory legal acts of the Republic of Belarus in the field of personal data protection.
1.3. By using the Application, the User expresses their consent to the terms of this Policy and grants the Administration consent to process their personal data in the manner, scope, and on the terms established by this Policy.
1.4. In case of disagreement with the terms of this Policy, the User must immediately cease using the Application and delete it from their device.
2. Terms and Definitions
2.1. Administration — the entity that is the owner of the Application and the operator of personal data.
2.2. User — an individual who has reached 18 (eighteen) years of age, registered in the Application, and uses its functionality.
2.3. Personal Data — any information relating to an identified individual or an individual who can be identified, in accordance with Article 1 of the Law on Personal Data.
2.4. Processing of Personal Data — any action (operation) or set of actions (operations) performed with personal data, including collection, systematization, storage, modification, use, depersonalization, blocking, distribution, provision, deletion of personal data.
2.5. Video Meeting (Vibe Session) — a real-time video communication session between two or more Users, organized through the functionality of the Application.
3. Principles of Personal Data Processing
The Administration processes personal data based on the following principles:
3.1. Legality — the processing of personal data is carried out in accordance with the legislation of the Republic of Belarus.
3.2. User Consent — the processing of personal data is carried out with the User's consent, except in cases provided for by law.
3.3. Proportionality — the processing of personal data is limited to achieving specific, pre-declared, and legitimate purposes. Processing of personal data incompatible with the purposes of their collection is not permitted.
3.4. Ensuring Security — the Administration takes necessary organizational and technical measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, destruction, alteration, blocking, copying, and distribution.
4. Categories of Processed Personal Data
The Administration processes the following categories of Users' personal data:
4.1. Registration Data
List of data:
•	Mobile phone number;
•	Email address (if available);
•	Name (nickname);
•	Date of birth (to confirm adulthood);
•	Gender.
Purpose of processing:
Creation and maintenance of an account, User identification, ensuring access security, age verification (18+).
4.2. Profile Data
List of data:
•	Photos (avatar, profile images);
•	Information about oneself (biography, interests, hobbies);
•	Selected topics for video meetings;
•	Other information voluntarily posted by the User in the profile.
Purpose of processing:
Formation of the User's profile for presentation to other Users, matching interlocutors based on specified interests and topics, providing functionality for dating and video meetings.
4.3. Technical Data
List of data:
•	IP address;
•	Device type (model, operating system);
•	Device identifiers (IDFA for iOS, Android ID for Android);
•	Session data (login time, logout time, session duration);
•	Connection quality data (data transfer speed, delays, packet loss);
•	Application version;
•	Information about errors and crashes (crash-report logs).
Purpose of processing:
Ensuring the functionality of the Application, diagnosing and resolving technical issues, providing technical support to Users, preventing fraud and unauthorized access, collecting anonymized statistics to improve the quality of the Application.
4.4. Communication Data
List of data:
•	Content of text messages (correspondence between Users);
•	Metadata of video meetings (session duration, date and time of start and end, participant identifiers);
•	Real-time video stream (not recorded or stored).
Purpose of processing:
Ensuring communication between Users (exchange of text messages, organization of video meetings).
Special mode of video data processing:
The video stream transmitted during video meetings is processed exclusively in real-time. The Administration does not record, save, or store the video stream. The detailed procedure for processing video data is set forth in Section 7 of this Policy.
4.5. Moderation Data
List of data:
•	Records of complaints submitted by Users (complaint date, violator's profile, description of the violation);
•	History of violations committed by the User (date, nature of violation, measures applied);
•	Screenshots, video recordings, or other evidence of violations provided by Users or obtained during automated moderation (if any);
•	Results of checking and reviewing complaints.
Purpose of processing:
Ensuring compliance with the User Agreement, moderation of content (profiles, text messages, video meetings), identification and suppression of illegal activities, dissemination of prohibited content (Nazi symbols, extremist materials, pornography, incitement of hatred), blocking violators, compliance with the requirements of the legislation of the Republic of Belarus.
Retention period for moderation data:
Moderation data is stored for 3 (three) years from the date of the last action related to the violation, after which it is subject to destruction or depersonalization. In case of transfer of materials to law enforcement authorities, the retention period is extended until the completion of the investigation or court proceedings.
4.6. Anonymized Data (not considered personal data)
In addition to the personal data specified in clauses 4.1–4.5, the Administration collects and processes anonymized statistical data that does not allow for the identification of a specific User:
•	Number of active Users;
•	Number of video meetings conducted;
•	Most popular meeting topics;
•	Average session duration;
•	Geographical distribution of Users (at the country or region level, without attribution to a specific individual);
•	Type of devices and operating systems used by Users.
Purpose of processing anonymized data:
Analyzing the effectiveness of the Application, planning feature development, compiling reports, improving user experience. Anonymized data is not personal data and is not subject to the Law of the Republic of Belarus "On Personal Data Protection".
5. Legal Grounds for Personal Data Processing
The Administration processes personal data based on at least one of the following grounds:
5.1. User Consent — the User gives consent to the processing of their personal data at the time of registration in the Application, as well as during the direct use of the Application. The consent is specific, informed, and conscious.
5.2. Performance of a Contract — processing is necessary for the performance of the User Agreement to which the User is a party.
5.3. Compliance with Legislation — processing is necessary to fulfill the requirements of the legislation of the Republic of Belarus (including for transferring information to law enforcement authorities upon their lawful request).
5.4. Protection of Life and Health — processing is necessary to protect the life, health, or other vital interests of the User or third parties.
6. User Consent to Personal Data Processing
6.1. By registering in the Application and checking the box indicating agreement with the terms of the User Agreement and this Privacy Policy, the User grants the Administration consent to process their personal data in the manner, scope, and on the terms established by this Policy.
6.2. The User's consent is valid for the entire duration of using the Application and for 5 (five) years after the cessation of use, unless otherwise provided by law.
7. Specifics of Video Meeting Processing
7.1. The Administration does not record, save, or store video meetings (Vibe Sessions) conducted by Users using the Application's functionality.
7.2. The video stream is transmitted between Users in real-time and is automatically deleted from the Administration's servers upon termination of the communication session. The Application's functionality does not provide for the possibility of recording video meetings by either Users or the Administration.
7.3. The Administration does not carry out preliminary moderation, listening to, or viewing of video meetings in real-time.
7.4. The Administration reserves the right to carry out technical monitoring of video sessions exclusively for the following purposes:
•	Collection of anonymized technical statistics (session duration, connection quality, termination code) without access to the content of the communication;
•	Processing of User complaints regarding the content and nature of video meetings, as well as taking appropriate measures to stop the use of the Application by Users who have violated the legislation of the Republic of Belarus;
•	Access to the video session is carried out in exceptional cases based on a reasoned request from an authorized state body of the Republic of Belarus in accordance with the procedure established by law, or in the presence of a court decision that has entered into legal force.
8. Purposes of Personal Data Processing
The Administration processes Users' personal data exclusively for the following purposes:
8.1. Ensuring the Functionality of the Application — registration and identification of the User, providing access to the Application's functionality, technical support.
8.2. Organizing Communication — enabling video meetings, text messaging, matching interlocutors based on selected topics and interests.
8.3. Moderation and Security Assurance — identifying and suppressing violations of the User Agreement and the legislation of the Republic of Belarus, including the dissemination of pornography, Nazi symbols, extremist materials, incitement of hatred, fraud.
8.4. Compliance with Legislation — fulfilling the requirements of the legislation of the Republic of Belarus, including the transfer of information to law enforcement authorities upon their lawful request.
8.5. Improving the Quality of the Application — collecting anonymized statistics to analyze Application usage, identify errors, and optimize performance.
8.6. Informing Users — sending notifications related to the operation of the Application (technical messages, responses to inquiries), as well as (with separate consent) marketing messages.
9. Procedure for Personal Data Processing
9.1. Collection of personal data is carried out:
•	When the User registers in the Application;
•	When filling out and editing the profile;
•	During the use of the Application's functionality (correspondence, video meetings);
•	When the User contacts the support service.
9.2. Storage of personal data is carried out on the Administration's servers located in the territory of the Republic of Belarus, as well as (if necessary) on servers of affiliates and partners providing the technical infrastructure of the Application (cloud services, hosting providers), subject to compliance with the requirements of legislation on cross-border transfer of personal data.
9.3. Retention periods for personal data:
•	Personal data is stored for the entire duration of the User's use of the Application.
•	After the account is deleted, personal data is stored for 5 (five) years to comply with legal requirements (tax, law enforcement) and resolve possible disputes.
•	After the expiration of the specified period, personal data is subject to destruction or depersonalization.
9.4. Destruction of personal data is carried out:
•	Upon achieving the purposes of processing or when the need for achieving them is lost;
•	Upon withdrawal of the User's consent to the processing of personal data (unless otherwise provided by law);
•	Upon request of an authorized state body in cases established by law.
9.5. Cross-border transfer of personal data is carried out in accordance with the requirements of the Law on Personal Data. The Administration notifies the authorized body (National Center for Personal Data Protection) of its intention to carry out cross-border transfer of personal data in accordance with the established procedure.

10.1. The Administration does not transfer Users' personal data to third parties, except in the following cases:
10.1.1. Technical Partners — transfer of personal data to organizations providing the technical infrastructure of the Application (hosting providers, cloud services, analytics services) based on contracts containing obligations to ensure the confidentiality and security of personal data.
10.1.2. Law Enforcement Authorities — transfer of personal data upon a reasoned request from a court, prosecutorial authorities, preliminary investigation bodies, inquest bodies, internal affairs bodies, the State Security Committee, the Ministry of Information of the Republic of Belarus in accordance with the procedure established by law.
