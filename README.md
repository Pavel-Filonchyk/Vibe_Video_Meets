PRIVACY POLICY

for the Vibe Video Meets (VVM) mobile app

1. General Provisions

1.1. This Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as the "Policy") is an integral part of the User Agreement for the Vibe Video Meets mobile app (hereinafter referred to as the "Application") and defines the processing and protection of Users' personal data, as well as other information received by the Application Administration.

1.2. This Policy has been developed in accordance with the Law of the Republic of Belarus dated May 7, 2021, No. 99-Z "On the Protection of Personal Data" (hereinafter referred to as the "Personal Data Law"), as well as other regulatory legal acts of the Republic of Belarus in the field of personal data protection.
1.3. By using the Application, the User agrees to the terms of this Policy and gives the Administration consent to the processing of their personal data in the manner, scope, and under the conditions established by this Policy.
1.4. If the User disagrees with the terms of this Policy, they must immediately stop using the Application and delete it from their device.
3. Terms and Definitions
2.1. Administration — the owner of the Application and the personal data controller.
2.2. User — an individual who has reached the age of 18 (eighteen) and who has registered in the Application and uses its functionality.
2.3. Personal data — any information related to an identified individual or an identifiable individual, in accordance with Article 1 of the Law on Personal Data.
2.4. Personal data processing — any action (operation) or set of actions (operations) performed with personal data, including collection, systematization, storage, modification, use, depersonalization, blocking, distribution, provision, and deletion of personal data.
2.5. A video meeting (Vibe session) is a real-time video conference between two or more Users, organized through the Application.
4. Principles of Personal Data Processing
The Administration processes personal data based on the following principles:
3.1. Legality - Personal data is processed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Belarus.
3.2. User Consent - Personal data is processed with the User's consent, except in cases stipulated by law.
3.3. Proportionality - Personal data processing is limited to achieving specific, pre-defined, and legitimate purposes. Processing of personal data incompatible with the purposes for which it was collected is prohibited.
3.4. Security - The Administration takes the necessary organizational and technical measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, destruction, modification, blocking, copying, and distribution.
5. Categories of Processed Personal Data
The Administration processes the following categories of Users' personal data:
4.1. Registration Data
List of Data:
• Mobile Phone Number;
• Email Address (if available);
• Name (Nickname);
• Date of Birth (to confirm age);
• Gender.
Purpose of Processing:
Account Creation and Maintenance, User Identification, Ensuring Access Security, Age Verification (18+).
4.2. Profile Data
List of Data:
• Photos (avatar, profile images);
• Personal Information (Biography, Interests, Hobbies);
• Selected Topics for Video Meetings;
• Other Information Voluntarily Posted by the User in the Profile.
Purpose of Processing:
Creating a User Profile for Presentation to Other Users, Selecting Interlocutors Based on Specified Interests and Topics, Providing Dating and Video Meeting Functionality.
4.3. Technical Data
List of Data:
• IP Address;
• Device Type (Model, Operating System);
• Device Identifiers (IDFA for iOS, Android ID for Android);
• Session data (App login time, logout time, session duration);
• Connection quality data (data transfer rate, latency, packet loss);
• App version;
• Error and crash information (crash report logs).
Purpose of processing:
Ensuring the functionality of the App, diagnosing and troubleshooting technical issues, providing technical support to Users, preventing fraud and unauthorized access, and collecting anonymized statistics to improve the quality of the App.
4.4. Communication Data
List of data:
• Text message content (correspondence between Users);
• Video meeting metadata (session duration, start and end date and time, participant IDs);
• Live video stream (not recorded or stored).
Purpose of processing:
Ensuring communication between Users (text messaging, organizing video meetings).
Special video processing mode:
The video stream transmitted during video meetings is processed exclusively in real time.
The Service does not record, save, or store video streams. Detailed procedures for processing video data are set out in Section 7 of this Policy.
4.5. Moderation Data
List of data:
• records of complaints submitted by Users (date of complaint, offender profile, description of the violation);
• history of violations committed by the User (date, nature of the violation, measures taken);
• screenshots, video recordings, or other evidence of violations provided by Users or obtained through automated moderation (if any);
• results of the verification and review of complaints.
Purpose of processing:
Ensuring compliance with the User Agreement, moderating content (profiles, text messages, video meetings), identifying and preventing illegal activity, dissemination of prohibited content (Nazi symbols, extremist materials, pornography, incitement to hatred), blocking violators, and complying with the laws of the Republic of Belarus.
Moderation Data Retention Period:
Moderation data is stored for three (3) years from the last violation-related action, after which it is subject to destruction or anonymization. If materials are transferred to law enforcement agencies, the storage period is extended until the end of the investigation or legal proceedings.
4.6. Anonymized Data (not considered personal data)
In addition to the personal data specified in paragraphs 4.1–4.5, the Administration collects and processes anonymized statistical data that does not allow identification of a specific User:
• Number of active Users;
• Number of video meetings held;
• Most popular meeting topics;
• Average session duration;
• Geographic distribution of Users (at the country or regional level, not linked to a specific individual);
• Type of devices and operating systems used by Users.
Purpose of processing anonymized data:
Analysis of the Application's performance, planning functionality development, reporting, and improving the user experience. Anonymized data is not considered personal data and is not subject to the Law of the Republic of Belarus "On the Protection of Personal Data."
6. Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data
Personal data is processed by the Administration if at least one of the following grounds exists:
5.1. User Consent - The User consents to the processing of their personal data upon registration in the Application, as well as through direct use of the Application. This consent is specific, informed, and conscious.
5.2. Contract Performance - Processing is necessary for the performance of the User Agreement to which the User is a party.
5.3. Compliance with Law - Processing is necessary to comply with the requirements of the laws of the Republic of Belarus (including the transfer of information to law enforcement agencies upon their legal request).
5.4. Protection of Life and Health - Processing is necessary to protect the life, health, or other vital interests of the User or third parties.
7. User Consent to the Processing of Personal Data
6.1. By registering in the Application and agreeing to the terms of the User Agreement and this Privacy Policy, the User consents to the Administration processing their personal data in the manner, scope, and under the terms established by this Policy.
6.2. The User's consent is valid for the entire term of use of the Application and for five (5) years after termination of use, unless otherwise provided by law.
8. Video Meeting Processing Features
7.1. The Administration does not record, save, or store video meetings (Vibe sessions) conducted by Users using the Application's functionality.
7.2. The video stream is transmitted between Users in real time and is automatically deleted from the Administration's servers at the end of the communication session. The Application's functionality does not provide the ability to record video meetings by either Users or the Administration.
7.3. The Administration does not pre-moderate, listen to, or view video meetings in real time.
7.4. The Administration reserves the right to conduct technical monitoring of video sessions solely for the following purposes:
• collecting anonymized technical statistics (session duration, connection quality, termination code) without access to the content of the communication;
• processing User complaints regarding the content and nature of video meetings, as well as taking appropriate measures to prevent the use of the Application by Users who violate the laws of the Republic of Belarus;
• access to a video session is granted in exceptional cases based on a justified request from an authorized state body of the Republic of Belarus in the manner prescribed by law, or in the presence of a final and binding court decision.
9. Purposes of personal data processing
The administration processes Users personal data exclusively for the following purposes:
8.1. Ensuring the functioning of the Application — User registration and identification, granting access to Application functionality, and providing technical support.
8.2. Organizing communication — enabling video meetings, text messaging, and matching users based on selected topics and interests.
8.3. Moderation and security — identifying and preventing violations of the User Agreement and the laws of the Republic of Belarus, including the distribution of pornography, Nazi symbols, extremist materials, incitement to hatred, and fraud.
8.4. Enforcement of the law — complying with the requirements of the laws of the Republic of Belarus, including transferring information to law enforcement agencies upon their legal request.
8.5. Improving the quality of the Application — collecting anonymized statistics to analyze Application usage, identify errors, and optimize performance.
8.6. Informing Users — sending notifications related to the operation of the Application (technical messages, responses to inquiries), as well as (with separate consent) marketing messages.
10. Personal Data Processing Procedure
9.1. Personal data is collected:
• When the User registers in the Application;
• When filling out and editing a profile;
• While using the Application's functionality (correspondence, video meetings);
• When the User contacts support.
9.2. Personal data is stored on the Administration's servers located in the Republic of Belarus, as well as (if necessary) on the servers of affiliates and partners providing the Application's technical infrastructure (cloud services, hosting providers), subject to compliance with the requirements of the legislation on the cross-border transfer of personal data.
9.3. Personal Data Retention Period:
• Personal data is stored for the entire period of the User's use of the Application.
• After account deletion, personal data is stored for 5 (five) years to comply with legal requirements (tax, law enforcement) and resolve potential disputes.
• After this period, personal data is subject to destruction or anonymization.
9.4. Personal data is destroyed:
• upon achievement of the processing objectives or when there is no longer a need to achieve them;
• upon revocation of the User's consent to the processing of personal data (unless otherwise provided by law);
• at the request of an authorized government agency in cases established by law.
9.5. Cross-border transfer of personal data is carried out in accordance with the requirements of the Law on Personal Data. The Administration notifies the authorized agency (the National Center for Personal Data Protection) of its intention to carry out cross-border transfer of personal data in accordance with the established procedure.
11. Transfer of personal data to third parties
10.1. The Administration does not transfer Users' personal data to third parties, except in the following cases:
10.1.1. Technical partners — transfer personal data to organizations providing the technical infrastructure of the Application (hosting providers, cloud services, analytics services) based on agreements containing obligations to ensure the confidentiality and security of personal data.
10.1.2. Law enforcement agencies — transfer personal data upon a justified request from a court, prosecutor's office, preliminary investigation, inquiry, internal affairs agencies, the State Security Committee, or the Ministry of Information of the Republic of Belarus in accordance with the procedure established by law.
10.1.3. Compliance with the law — transfer personal data in other cases expressly provided for by the legislation of the Republic of Belarus.
